* Data Flow: I/O
** Outbound
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|
| Definition       | refers to data provided by the "back office" to the field. |
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|
| change frequency | rather high (>weekly)                                      |
| modularity       | as low as possibe ("one app" ideal)                        |
| recovery         | backed up upstream (e.g. POC)                              |
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|

+ (dates and field activities)
+ sample locations
  + core location (-> navigation)
  + [[microlocation]] = target placement at sampling area (e.g. aquatic types, biotic sample field)
  + replacements (/cf./ [[replacement cells]])


** Inbound
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|
| Definition       | Data captured in the field, stored for analysis.           |
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|
| change frequency | minimal (captured as-is; could be "append only")           |
| modularity       | partially given by INBO-context interactions (e.g. WATINA) |
| recovery         | "all in" (daily diffs, weekly dumps)                       |
|------------------+------------------------------------------------------------|
